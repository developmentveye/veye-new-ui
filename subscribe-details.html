With the ever changing landscape of AI development and corresponding emerging technologies come a lot of opportunities, but also a lot of risk. The machine learning and data driven AI are transforming the landscape of industries. According to a report by Grand View Research, the global artificial intelligence market size, estimated at USD 196.63 billion in 2023 is projected to grow at a CAGR of 36.6% from 2024 to 2030.  Although analysts differ on the AI market growth, there is complete unanimity on its growth at a CAGR exceeding 30%. The investment return potential in AI can be easily gauged by the growth of stocks like Nvidia. It almost tripled in 2024 while continuing to rise 12% in November alone. As per market experts, the Silicon Valley chip maker became the first company in history to overcome a stock market value of $3.6 trillion While we rapidly approach the inflection point of technology, retail individual investors may look no further to separate the contenders from pretenders.
